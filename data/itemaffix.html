Item affix

From Path of Exile Wiki
(Redirected from Item Affix)
Jump to: navigation, search
For jewel affixes, see Jewel.

Equipment can be found in magic and rare rarities, being imbued with special modifications that grant passive buffs to their wielder. These modifications are added with affixes, sorted into prefixes and suffixes. A magic item may have up to one prefix and one suffix, while a rare may have up to three of each. Unique items have custom modifications and do not have affixes.

The name of a magic item contains the prefix and suffix modifications applied to it. For example, a Long Bow with the "Frosted" prefix and the "of Impact" suffix would be named "Frosted Long Bow of Impact". Rare items have their names randomly generated from a list of rare names and do not follow this system.

Magic items can only have a prefix and suffix, so if they are already named X Item of Y, you can't use Orbs of Augmentation on them. Exalted Orbs can be used on rare items with fewer than six affixes.

All affixes have a level associated with them, and will only appear on items with an item level greater than or equal to the modifier's level.

Information taken from the Path of Exile website. Note that item limitations were observed in game and by searching poe.xyz.is, so there may be errors.

Modifier Explanations

Local modifiers only affect the item they appear on, for instance the "Vicious" mod (Local Physical Damage +% 90–109) This modifier on a sword will only affect the weapon's base damage. It won't affect the damage on an off-hand weapon when dual-wielding, and won't increase physical damage added from other items like rings. "Local" is found on weapons and armour, but "global" appears on all items.

A modifier will increase the required level of an item to 80% of the modifier's level if that value is greater than the level requirement for the base item.
